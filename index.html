Abigail O'Regan is a Sophomore at VCUarts, majoring in Painting and Printmaking with a minor in Art History. She specializes in traditional and mixed-media painting, focusing on themes of love, beauty, loss, and nature. Abigail's passion for art is deeply rooted in her academic journey and passion for social causes, where she continues to refine her technical skills to develop an artistic voice. In addition to her studio work, Abigail's commissions include murals, allowing her to bring her creative voice and commentary on public causes like environmentalism and social concerns. A desire to create work that resonates with others and provokes meaningful discussions drives her creative journey.
